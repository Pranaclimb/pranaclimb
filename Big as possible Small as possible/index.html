🧪 The Science Behind the Cue

Fagevik Olsén et al. (2011) discovered that the words we use to cue breathing directly affect chest expansion:

Traditional Cue

"Breathe in maximally"

"Breathe out maximally"

✅ Modified Cue

"Breathe in maximally and make yourself as BIG as possible"

"Breathe out maximally and make yourself as SMALL as possible"

+1.4 cm upper ribcage
+0.9 cm lower ribcage

Key Insight: Same breath. Different cue. Better mobility.

How we cue the breath changes how the body responds. This is crucial for climbing coaching and breathwork training.
