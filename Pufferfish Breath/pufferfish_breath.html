How to Do It

1

Soft Nasal Inhale

Breathe in gently through your nose. No tension—just enough air to lightly puff your cheeks like a pufferfish.

2

Brief Hold (0.5–1 sec)

Gentle pressurization with puffed cheeks. Never strain. This is a soft reset, not a power breath.

3

Slow Controlled Exhale

Let your cheeks deflate naturally. Lips relaxed, long sigh-like release through the mouth.

4

Repeat 1–3 Times

Do NOT hyperventilate. This is a reset breath, not a pump. Use after cruxes to normalize your system.
