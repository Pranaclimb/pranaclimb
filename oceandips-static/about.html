About Annie

Ocean addict. Climber. Breathwork coach. Cold water swimmer. Founder of Pranaclimb.

Annie Anderson is an independent researcher, breathwork specialist, and lifelong climber with over 30 years of worldwide experience in climbing, yoga, and performance science.

She is the creator of the Pranaclimb Methodology — a field-adapted system that uses breathing rate, perceived exertion, and heart-rate recovery to track performance thresholds and recovery in real-time.

She lives with her husband Mark and daughter Annalisa beside the sea cliffs of Cornwall, where she climbs, swims daily in the icy Atlantic, and practises breathwork and yoga year-round.

🧗 Qualified Climbing Instructor (Plas-Y-Brenin 1998) 🧘 Yoga Teacher RYT 200 (Istanbul 2012) 🫁 Breathwork Specialist 🌊 Cold Water Coach 🔬 Researcher · Respiratory Physiology & Recovery Science ⛑️ Qualified First Aider 💪 Stage 4 Cancer Survivor

The Story

Her philosophy — live, love life every day — carried her through three years of Stage 4 Renal Cell Carcinoma and surgery.

Family, friends, the ocean, climbing, breathwork, cold water, positivity, and gratitude continue to inspire every moment.

Cold water swimming was a powerful healer on Annie's journey through Stage 4 Kidney Cancer. She experienced first-hand the transformative power of the ocean — physically, mentally, and spiritually — and now shares that with others through Ocean Dips.

🌊 Ocean Dips

Guided wild cold-water and coastal walking experiences along the breathtaking coastline of Gwithian, Godrevy, and West Cornwall. Each dip is shaped by tide, weather, and light.

🫁 Pranaclimb

A breath-led climbing performance system bridging sports physiology and practical coaching. Annie's research has been published on SportRxiv and ResearchGate.

📖 The Book

Author of Pranaclimb: Love, Breathe, Climb — combining science, breathwork, and elite insights, offering unique practices for climbers.

🏡 Cornwall

Living behind the dunes of Gwithian, Annie swims daily in the Atlantic, walks the coastal paths barefoot, and shares the magic of this wild coastline with every guest.

🌊 The Philosophy

These are not just dips. They are immersive experiences in nature, designed to reconnect you with your breath, your body, and the ocean.

Annie believes that cold water, breath, and movement are three of the most powerful tools we have for resetting the nervous system, building resilience, and reconnecting with what matters.

Ocean Dips is the natural extension of everything Annie has lived, researched, and practised — bringing the Pranaclimb philosophy to the ocean and the wild Cornish coast.

Every experience is personal, guided, and shaped around the rhythm of the tides. No fixed schedules. No pressure. Just presence.
