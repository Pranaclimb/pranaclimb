🌊 The Experience

A full Pranaclimb-style day combining:

  • Coastal walking along Rinsey Head
  • Climbing at Trewavas Head
  • Breathwork
  • Guided dip in the stunning Ray Pool
  • Recovery, warm-up & integration

The Ray Pool is one of the most beautiful natural tidal pools on the Cornish coast — a hidden gem that rewards those who make the journey.

A good level of fitness is recommended for this full-day experience.
