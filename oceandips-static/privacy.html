Privacy Policy

How Ocean Dips collects, uses and protects your personal data.

Last updated: April 2026 · Ocean Dips is a trading name of Pranaclimb Ltd

1. Who We Are

Ocean Dips is a trading name of Pranaclimb Ltd. We are the data controller for personal information collected through oceandips.com and our booking system. Our contact email is annie@pranaclimb.com.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect personal data when you book an experience, contact us by email, or use our website. This may include:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your phone number (if provided)
  • Medical information disclosed at the time of booking
  • Payment information (processed securely by Stripe or PayPal — we do not store card details)
  • Communication history (emails between you and Annie)

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  • Process and manage your booking
  • Send booking confirmations and joining instructions
  • Contact you about your session (weather updates, rescheduling etc.)
  • Ensure your safety during your experience
  • Comply with our legal and insurance obligations

We will never sell your data to third parties or use it for purposes other than those listed above without your explicit consent.

4. Medical Information

Any medical information you disclose is used solely to ensure your safety during your Ocean Dips experience. This information is treated with the strictest confidence and is not shared with any third party except where required by law or emergency services.

5. Third Party Services

We use the following third party services which may process your data:

  • Calendly — booking management (calendly.com/privacy)
  • Stripe — payment processing (stripe.com/privacy)
  • PayPal — payment processing (paypal.com/privacy)
  • Cloudflare — website hosting and security (cloudflare.com/privacy)

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

6. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies. Cloudflare may set security cookies to protect the site from malicious traffic. Calendly and Stripe may also set cookies when you use their booking and payment services. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with our legal obligations. Booking records are typically retained for 7 years for accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting annie@pranaclimb.com.

8. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights please contact annie@pranaclimb.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages with SSL encryption. Payment data is processed entirely by Stripe and PayPal and is never stored by Ocean Dips.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version will always be available at oceandips.com/privacy. We will notify you of any significant changes by email where appropriate.
